Burgers and rice

(back)

Description

Burgers, and also rice. Doesn't actually look like the above.

Ingredients

  • burgers
  • basmati rice
  • ghee or butter
  • vegetable stock

Steps

  1. Melt butter or ghee in a small pan
  2. Add rice
  3. Move rice around a bit then let simmer for ~15 mins
  4. Also cook the burgers
  5. Put the burgers on the rice once it's all cooked
